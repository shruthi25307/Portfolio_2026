A responsive transportation management portal developed to streamline college bus-related information and communication.

CBUS is a web-based college transportation portal designed to provide students and staff with easy access to bus routes, schedules, announcements, transport updates, and driver contact information through a centralized and user-friendly platform.

Key Features

  • Bus route information and route listings
  • Bus tracking interface
  • Transport schedules and travel details

  • Responsive design for all devices
